The Technology

How does Product-Centric Diffusion work?

Unlike generic generative AI, GaanaAI utilizes a specialized architecture explicitly designed for e-commerce and product integrity.

Product-Centric Diffusion is a specialized machine learning pipeline that trains custom AI models exclusively on the unique geometry, typography, and branding of a physical product. Unlike generic text-to-video models, this architecture ensures:

  • Zero Hallucinations: Eliminates the warping and morphing typically seen in general-purpose AI video generators.
  • Legible Typography: Maintains perfect clarity for package text, logos, and intricate product labels.
  • Accurate Physics: Reflects light off surfaces (e.g., glass, metallic, matte) with 100% physical fidelity.
  • Environment Grounding: Simulates complex 3D cinematic lighting without ever distorting the product's physical reality.

Consequently, modern brands can achieve high-fidelity, agency-grade creative output at a fraction of traditional production costs.

Everything you need to know about adding video to your product pages.

What is a PDP video and why does my product page need one?
+
A PDP video sits on your product detail page in place of (or alongside) the static product image. Shoppers who watch a product video are far more likely to add to cart — product pages with video typically see up to 50% higher conversion than image-only pages.
Will the videos work on my product pages and marketplaces?
+
Yes. Every video is delivered ready to drop straight into your PDPs — your own store (Shopify or custom), plus Amazon, Flipkart, and Walmart — in the exact aspect ratios and formats each platform requires.
Do the PDP videos match my product and brand exactly?
+
Yes. Every video matches your product, packaging, colors, and brand styling, so what shoppers see on the page is a true representation of what ships — no distortion, no off-brand surprises.
How much can a video lift conversion and engagement on a PDP?
+
Brands typically see up to 50% higher conversion and significantly longer time on page when a product page has video instead of a static image. Video keeps shoppers engaged and answers the questions a photo can't.
What do I need to send to get a PDP video?
+
Just your product page URL (or your product photos) and any brand guidelines. We turn that into a finished, page-ready video — no shoots and no editing on your side.
How fast will my PDP videos be ready?
+
Standard turnaround is 24 hours, at any catalog scale — so you can roll video out across your product pages as fast as you can list them.
